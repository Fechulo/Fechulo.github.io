Introduction

I thought it could be a fun idea to compile a series of screenshots and stories from behind the scenes of the plugin’s development and create a space to share them with anyone who’s interested

Background

Back in the day, when the UK Controller Plugin was first introduced in VATUK, VATéir was given permission to use it. As the UKCP feature list grew, a lot of its functionality became very UK-focused, so it eventually became obsolete for VATéir controllers.

Then one day, Paul had a vision of a EuroScope plugin for designed specifically for VATéir controllers. Paul’s idea of a VATéir plugin was simple; something which would automate a lot of the tedious and repetitive tasks that controllers have to carry out while controlling, such as assigning squawks and setting initial climbs. But he also had a lot of bigger long-term ideas for a much more advanced piece of software that would change the way we control in VATéir.

So on the 11th of November 2020, Paul approached me with the opportunity to work on his vision. I had never worked on a project like this and only knew a little bit of C++, but I thought I’d give it a go. After a few days of sorting through EuroScope documentation and trying out bits of code from StackOverflow, I had a working prototype. So I officially accepted the challenge and the rest is history!

Firstly, thanks to Paul and the rest of the VATéir Staff for giving me the opportunity to give back to the vACC in such a meaningful way.

Naming The Plugin

It took quite a long time to settle on a name for the plugin. For most of it’s early development, it was called VATéirPlugin. In fact, a lot of the plugin’s code still makes reference to that name. I consulted with the staff on numerous occasions and a number of suggestions were made. Some of them were not family-friendly and as such will not be listed here.

Here are some of the suggestions that didn’t make the cut:

  • VATéir Controller Assistant (VCA)

  • Irish Controller Plugin (ICP)

  • Sugar Loaf Avoidance Plugin (SLAP)

  • Nacho’s Terror Spy Bot (NTSB)

After a democratic survey, the current name of VATéir Controller Plugin was elected. However, that was too long to write/say constantly so we needed a short version. Many more discussions and another survey later, VÉCP became the official acronym.

Fonts

Up until v0.5.3, VÉCP used the default EuroScope font for all on-screen elements. While trying to patch some paintbrush-related leaks, I found out I could set a specific font for the plugin, so I went on a little trip down the Microsoft Font Shop. Here are some of the results:

One of the requirements I needed the final font to meet, was for it to be a monospace font, where the width of all characters is the same. Otherwise, things would get misaligned (as can be seen in the Comic Sans example above) and my OCD couldn’t cope with that.
